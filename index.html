How FoodBridge Works

Our platform makes food donation simple, efficient, and impactful.

List Surplus Food

Restaurants, event organizers, and households can easily list their surplus food with details.

NGOs Find Donations

NGOs browse available food listings and request pickups based on their needs.

Coordinate Pickup

Use our real-time chat system to coordinate pickup details and logistics.

Powerful Features

Everything you need to make food donation efficient and impactful.

  • Real-time notifications for new donations
  • Interactive map to find nearby donors and NGOs
  • Secure messaging system for coordination
  • Donation tracking and history
  • Verification system for quality assurance
